My Story

As a little girl, all I wanted to be was a software engineer. I started college at UC Santa Barbara with this purpose but parted ways with that dream. Ten years later after being on the business side of tech, participating in hack days, and spending most of my hours in the engineering cave, my heart and brain never stopped longing for that dream. So I quit my job and fully committed to making that childhood dream happen. I absolutely love tackling back-end coding challenges and meeting it with beautiful design.
